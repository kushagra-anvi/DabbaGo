Shipping Policy

Last updated: May 6, 2026

1. Delivery Areas

DabbaGo currently operates across major metropolitan areas in India including Delhi NCR, Mumbai, Bangalore, Pune, and Hyderabad. We are rapidly expanding our reach. Please use our location checker on the home page to see if we deliver to your specific pincode.

2. Delivery Timings

To ensure you receive your meal fresh and hot, we have strictly defined delivery slots:

  • Lunch: Delivered between 12:00 PM and 1:30 PM.
  • Dinner: Delivered between 7:30 PM and 9:00 PM.

We strive for 100% on-time delivery. However, timings may vary slightly due to weather conditions or extreme traffic.

3. Delivery Fees

At DabbaGo, we believe in transparent pricing. **All our subscription plans include free daily delivery.** There are no hidden charges or per-meal delivery fees.

4. Non-Delivery

In case of unforeseen circumstances (natural disasters, strikes, etc.) where delivery is not possible, your subscription will be automatically extended by one day, or a credit will be added to your account.

5. Delivery Tracking

Once your dabba is out for delivery, you will receive an SMS with a tracking link. You can also track the real-time status of your rider through our "Order Status" page.
